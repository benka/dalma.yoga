Yoga and Meditation with Dalma

Yoga is a Lifestyle, a Path.

It has an amazing effect on the Body and the Mind.

Teaches us how to become master of both.

Dalma Bekes

Yoga teacher

Yoga was always part of my life in some way.

Before I started to teach yoga I earned a BA degree as a recreational organizer and got professional fundamentals of anatomy, physiology, training theory, leisure activities among many others.
I gained many years of experience working as a group fitness instructor and personal trainer before I became a yoga teacher in 2012.

But studying is a never ending process. I constantly extend my knowledge by attending different courses and workshops plus reading lots of books :)  In the past few years I completed courses such as Pilates,  yoga nidra and Teaching Meditation. And of course I learn throughout my own practice. Especially meditation, it became really important to me.

Yoga means much more to me than a way of exercising.

The way I teach is gentle and caring. I keep advising my students to listen to their own body and only do what feels right.

Originally I completed a Vinyasa Flow teacher training but I don’t teach it at all. I have been influenced by Satyananda yoga and I love the more gentle, slower, traditional, meditative approach. I don't really care about “pretzel position” rather I care about to learn to practice each posture with full awareness, being in the present moment just like meditation.

I like to bring in extras to my classes to create a more relaxing environment. Things like candle light, incenses, singing bowls...

Private sessions

Personalised for your needs

I offer private sessions in the comfort of your own home. I teach yoga with different approaches according to your needs (injuries, sport specific yoga etc).

If you are interested in yoga deeper I can add special techniques like yoga Nidra (guided relaxation), meditation and sound bath.

I offer pre and post-natal yoga as well. Every woman should practice pregnancy yoga which will help a lot not just during labour but also to be physically and mentally ready for their new wonderful role as a mother. Post-natal yoga is equally important to fasten the recovery and many more.

If you have any questions or would like to arrange a trial session feel free to contact me
